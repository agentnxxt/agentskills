We've been quietly building something powerful inside Autonomyx.

Not just products. Not just services. A library of AI skills — purpose-built, battle-tested agents that make Claude dramatically smarter at specific jobs. And today, we're releasing the first one to the world.

Meet the Website Policy Drafting Skill.

What Is an AI Skill?

Think of it like this: Claude is brilliant out of the box. But a skill is what happens when you give Claude deep domain expertise, a structured workflow, and institutional knowledge — all baked into a single, installable file.

It's the difference between asking a smart generalist and talking to a specialist who's done this a thousand times.

Our Website Policy Drafting Skill turns Claude into exactly that — a specialist compliance advisor for founders, developers, and product teams.

The Problem It Solves

You're building something. A SaaS. A mobile app. An AI product. A marketplace.

At some point, someone asks: "Do we have a privacy policy?"

And then begins the scramble. Legal templates that don't fit. Lawyers that cost thousands. Generic policy generators that spit out boilerplate no one reads. Regulations you've never heard of — GDPR, CCPA, DPDP, EU AI Act — that may or may not apply to your product.

It's a mess. And it slows builders down.

Tell it what you're building — your stack, your users, your integrations — and it figures out the rest. What regulations apply to you. What policies you need. In what order. Then it drafts them, publication-ready, right in your conversation.

This isn't a template filler. It's a compliance workflow, end to end.
